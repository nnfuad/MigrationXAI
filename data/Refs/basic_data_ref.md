**COL 6 — Overseas_Migrants_Per_1000 | Rows 1 and 18**

**Row 1 (Dhaka, 1.0):**
**Source:** IOM/DTM *Bangladesh Migration Snapshot Report 2023* (https://dtm.iom.int/sites/g/files/tmzbdl1461/files/reports/BGD%20Migration%20Snapshot%202023_Final.pdf), citing BMET 2024 data. Dhaka district: ~35,472 workers registered for overseas employment. **Row 18 (Cumilla, 17.6):**
**Source:** Same IOM/DTM *Bangladesh Migration Snapshot Report 2023*, citing BMET 2024: *"Cumilla emerges as a major district of origin with approximately 108,870 individuals seeking employment abroad."* Denominator: population 6,212,216 from BBS Census 2022. **Calculation:** 108,870 / 6,212,216 × 1,000 ≈ 17.6. Corroborated by The Daily Star (https://www.thedailystar.net/nrb/migration/news/bangladesh-tops-sea-arrivals-italy-early-2025-3874536).
